Cats Going on a Picnic

I came across this photo the other day and I really liked it. It symbolises me, if I were a cat and I went on a picnic with someone I liked. I think I would be the ginger cat, because it's a little bit cuter than the white cat. The ginger cat is also fluffier and I like the way it's holding onto its feet. This picture is so cute because the cats aren't really anywhere special, you can see in the background that they're just in a field or something. It doesn't matter where the cats are, they are just enjoying each other's company. I find that really cute. I also like that they are sharing a whole entire cake between them. It's way too much food for two little cats in the middle of the day.
